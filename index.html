I'm Seth, a Director of Data Analytics based in South Florida, specializing in building high-performing teams and modern data platforms that drive real business impact.

I partner with executives and business leaders who are ready to invest in their data capabilities and differentiate themselves through analytics-driven decision making.

Together we architect scalable solutions—from Snowflake data platforms to AI-powered workflows—that unlock millions in productivity and transform how organizations operate.

Currently, I lead a cross-functional data science and analytics team at Lennar, supporting Land Acquisition, Development, Construction, Customer Care, and Finance. I also head our Palantir Foundry Center of Excellence, enabling AI use cases that have unlocked over $50M in value.

Before that, I cut my teeth in process improvement at Kaplan, coaching Six Sigma teams and building data-driven solutions.

My background in Industrial/Organizational Psychology gives me a unique lens—I think about data not just as numbers, but as a tool for understanding human behavior and driving organizational change.
